Simple plugin to keep your post titles safe and sound!

==Description==
Simple, but effective gizmo has several useful functions for your blog. It can:

1. Remove the full stop from the end of the post title. 

2. Capitalise first letter of the title. 

3. Add space after the comma.

With this plugin you won't have to make these awful post revisions and finally be more confident about your content managers. 

by Natalie Andersen and Get Good Grade Team

==Features==

Removes the full stop from the end, capitalizes first letter of the header, adds spaces after comma

Installation:
1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the Settings->Plugin Name screen to configure the plugin
